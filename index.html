Biography

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, he is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay made his debut as a child actor in the Tamil film 'Vetri' (1984) and played his first lead role in 'Naalaiya Theerpu' (1992) at the age of 18. Over the years, he has become one of the most commercially successful actors in Tamil cinema, earning the nickname 'Thalapathy' (Commander) among his fans.
